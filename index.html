Research Interests

My research interests are understanding human interaction with the world and human skill transfer learning (e.g. to robots) either from a single human video or large video data. My works have been focusing on hand-object interaction both in 2D and 3D, and we have released several important datasets in the field: 100DOH (100 Days of Hand), VISOR and Hands23. I am broadly interested in research on video understanding, 3D reconstruction, and generating creative content.
